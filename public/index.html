About Mel

With almost a decade worth of experience in the beauty industry, I am UK-based professional makeup artist known for her flawless artistry, refined technique, and ability to bring a touch of luxury to every look.

Trained at the prestigious London Academy Freelance Makeup, I specialised in airbrush makeup, editorial beauty, film and television, and bespoke bridal artistry.

My work blends the precision of high-fashion makeup with the warmth and authenticity that make every client feel confident, radiant, and camera-ready.

Whether she's creating timeless elegance for a bride, sculpturing an essential look for a photoshoot or preparing talent for the screen, my signature approach combines technical mastery with an intuitive understanding of individual beauty.

Available for projects across the UK and internationally.
